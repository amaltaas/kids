3+ Language learning for young kids

This delightful little app arranges letters as a sequence of cards. Each card is illustrated and is accompanied by audio for pronunciation. The interface is so easy, even your child can use it.

  • 4 sets of cards: Alphabet, Animals, Colors and Numbers
  • Look up a word's pronunciation and meaning
  • Record your own voice to replace the built-in audio
  • Use 'Autoplay' to automatically move to the next card after a delay
  • Recommend your friends to download the app
  • Clean and beautiful design
  • No ads

Available in the following languages

  • Hindi
  • Marathi
  • Punjabi
